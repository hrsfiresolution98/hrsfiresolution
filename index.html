Fire Extinguisher

Used to control fire

Fire extinguishers are essential fire safety tools used to control or extinguish small fires in emergency situations. These devices come in various types such as CO2, Dry Powder, and Foam extinguishers, each designed for specific fire classes. They are easy to use and highly effective in preventing fire from spreading. Fire extinguishers are widely used in homes, offices, factories, and commercial buildings to ensure safety and quick response during fire incidents.
